Permitted pathways

Five governed offering pathways

Each pathway is defined in the governed offerings model and bounded by the Strategic Availability Protocol and Premium Brief Boundary.

  • Compute Sovereignty Brief — scoped synthesis of layered dependency exposure within a declared entity, sector, or deployment context.
  • Chip Dependency Audit — governed dependency-record review mapped to the Chip Dependency Map model.
  • Premium Reference Dossier — deep, sourced reference artifact for durable institutional use.
  • Ontology and Methodology Licensing — licensed use of governed ontology, methodology, and standard elements under written terms.
  • Strategic Acquisition Inquiry — qualified channel for institutions evaluating strategic interest in the asset.
Request boundary

What can be requested

Clients may define a question, scope, layers, time horizon, and intended use. Work follows the same source, claim, and confidence standards as the public reference surface.

Can request

Scoped briefs, audits, dossiers, licensing discussions, and serious strategic acquisition dialogue where evidence, scope, and governance boundaries can be declared upfront.

Cannot purchase

Predetermined conclusions, sovereignty assignments, rankings, benchmarks, certifications, guarantees, investment or legal advice, or official accreditation.

Evidence rule

Insufficient evidence produces no operational finding. Paid work does not relax registry scope, freshness review, or claim-class separation.

01

No predetermined conclusions

A client may commission scoped analysis, but cannot buy a preferred verdict. Public and private work follow the same source and claim policies, and material commercial relationships must be disclosed.

02

No public ranking or scoring

This layer does not offer a sovereignty score, benchmark, league table, certification, or outcome guarantee. The Compute Sovereignty Standard prohibits collapsing layered exposure into one headline number.

03

Evidence and confidence boundaries

Deliverables separate evidence, analysis, confidence, unknowns, limitations, and as-of dating. Registry-linked sources remain claim-specific; registry presence does not automatically create a finding.

04

Acquisition value preserved

The durable asset is the governed category, ontology, diagnostic method, source registry, and reference surface — not a sales funnel. Strategic availability makes those components visible and licensable without degrading neutrality.
