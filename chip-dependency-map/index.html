Compute Dependency Exposure Map

A future governed output that makes dependencies, evidence confidence, and unknowns visible without reducing them to a universal score.

Inputs

The model will ask about entity type, design control, external manufacturing, memory access, interconnect, power and cooling, access controls, supply alternatives, and deployment structure.

02

Governed output

The output will identify dependency layers, exposure types, evidence confidence, unknowns, and a suggested brief type. It will remain a preliminary diagnosis.

03

What it will not do

The tool will not assign an arbitrary universal score, make unsupported operational claims, or present missing information as evidence of weakness.
