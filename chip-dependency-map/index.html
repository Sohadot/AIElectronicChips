01

Diagnostic questions

Twelve bounded questions cover all eight layers. A response directs investigation; it is not itself a finding. Unknown and not-applicable responses remain visible.

02

Six exposure types

The model distinguishes concentration, access, capacity, technology, operational, and evidence exposure. Multiple exposure types may apply to one dependency.

03

Four confidence levels

High, medium, low, and insufficient confidence describe evidence quality and completeness. Insufficient confidence supports no operational finding.

04

Four bounded artifact outputs

The model may classify a Compute Sovereignty Brief, Chip Dependency Audit, Infrastructure Readiness Brief, or Premium Reference Dossier as the appropriate next analysis format. This is not a purchase, investment, deployment, or operational recommendation.

05

Protocol before interface

The machine-readable model, dependency record schema, validation script, and protocol establish governance before any future interaction layer is considered.
