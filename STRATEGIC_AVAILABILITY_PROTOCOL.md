# Strategic Availability Protocol

## Purpose

The Strategic Availability layer defines how AIElectronicChips.com extends its
public reference value into serious commercial pathways without weakening trust,
neutrality, or acquisition value.

It is not a storefront, checkout flow, advertising surface, or consulting pitch
page. It is a governed engagement boundary for premium briefs, scoped audits,
reference dossiers, ontology licensing, and strategic acquisition inquiry.

## Permitted Pathways

The machine-readable offering model in `data/strategic-offerings.json` defines
five permitted pathways:

1. **Compute Sovereignty Brief** — scoped synthesis within declared layers and
   time horizon.
2. **Chip Dependency Audit** — governed dependency-record review against the
   Chip Dependency Map model.
3. **Premium Reference Dossier** — deep, sourced reference artifact for
   institutional use.
4. **Ontology and Methodology Licensing** — licensed use of governed ontology,
   methodology, and standard elements under written terms.
5. **Strategic Acquisition Inquiry** — qualified channel for institutions
   evaluating strategic interest in the asset.

Each pathway has explicit purpose, inputs, deliverable boundaries, evidence
basis, prohibited outputs, and trust constraints.

## Excluded Commercial Models

The following are excluded from this layer and from public presentation:

- prices, quotes, or checkout flows on the public site;
- product rankings, benchmarks, or league tables;
- sovereignty scores, certifications, guarantees, or maturity grades;
- affiliate links, product reviews, sponsored conclusions, or display
  advertising;
- investment, legal, procurement, or national-security advice;
- claims of official accreditation, government endorsement, or industry
  certification;
- promises of predetermined conclusions or guaranteed outcomes.

These exclusions align with `MONETIZATION_BOUNDARY.md` and the Compute
Sovereignty Standard.

## Inquiry Handling

Serious inquiries are initiated through qualified contact, not self-serve
purchase.

A useful inquiry should state:

- the offering pathway or pathways of interest;
- the entity, sector, or topic;
- decision context and intended use;
- time horizon and relevant sovereignty layers;
- geographic scope and confidentiality boundaries;
- known evidence limits or exclusions.

Vague, promotional, or conclusion-seeking contacts may be declined. Inquiry
acceptance does not imply that every requested output can be delivered; scope
and evidence limits are confirmed before work begins.

## Source and Claim Governance for Paid Work

Public and private work follow the same standards:

- `SOURCE_POLICY.md` and `SOURCE_REGISTRY_PROTOCOL.md` govern evidence selection
  and registry use;
- `CLAIM_POLICY.md` governs claim classes and attribution;
- `COMPUTE_SOVEREIGNTY_STANDARD.md` governs interpretation, confidence, and
  prohibited outputs;
- `CHIP_DEPENDENCY_MAP_PROTOCOL.md` governs audit structure where applicable.

Paid work does not relax evidence requirements. Registry presence does not
automatically create a finding. Insufficient evidence produces no operational
finding.

## Commercial Relationship Disclosure

Material commercial relationships that could affect evidence selection,
interpretation, or publication must be disclosed in the relevant deliverable and
in any public artifact derived from commissioned work.

Clients may define a question and receive scoped analysis, but may not purchase
a predetermined conclusion. Sponsored or promotional influence must not alter
source selection, rankings, or confidence labels.

## Acquisition Value

This layer supports strategic acquisition value by making the asset's durable
components visible and licensable:

- a defensible category and vocabulary;
- a governed dependency ontology and diagnostic method;
- a sourced reference library and registry;
- bounded pathways to briefs, audits, dossiers, and licensing;
- explicit trust constraints that protect neutrality.

The public site therefore presents serious pathways without turning the
reference surface into a sales funnel or degrading its independence.
