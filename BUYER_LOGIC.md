# Buyer Logic

## Intended Users

The asset serves decision-makers who need to understand physical AI compute
dependencies, including:

- AI infrastructure and chip-design companies;
- cloud, data-center, and compute operators;
- semiconductor intelligence and supply-chain advisory firms;
- sovereign funds, institutional investors, and strategy teams;
- geopolitical, policy, and economic research organizations.

## Jobs To Be Done

Users may need to:

- identify hidden dependencies before investment or procurement;
- understand exposure across the sovereignty stack;
- compare deployment options without relying on vendor narratives;
- brief executives on infrastructure, policy, and supply-chain constraints;
- commission a focused audit or reference dossier.

## Why This Asset Matters

The durable value is not the domain name alone. It is the combined ownership of:

- a defensible category and vocabulary;
- a governed dependency ontology;
- a transparent diagnostic method;
- a sourced reference library;
- an interface built around the category;
- pathways to briefs, audits, licensing, and future data products.

## Trust Requirement

Buyer value depends on neutrality, evidence, and methodological transparency.
Commercial relationships must never silently alter findings, rankings, or
source selection.
