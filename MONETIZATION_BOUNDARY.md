# Monetization Boundary

## Principle

Revenue must extend the asset's reference value without degrading its
independence. Trust is a product requirement, not a marketing claim.

## Permitted Models

- paid Compute Sovereignty Briefs;
- scoped Chip Dependency Audits;
- AI Infrastructure Risk Briefs;
- premium reference dossiers;
- licensing of the ontology, methodology, or future standard;
- carefully governed API access after sufficient data maturity;
- strategic pilots and institutional partnerships with disclosed boundaries.

## Excluded Models

- low-quality display advertising;
- indiscriminate affiliate links;
- paid product rankings or reviews;
- sponsored conclusions;
- high-volume shallow content;
- sale of personal or confidential assessment data.

## Independence Rules

Commercial clients may define a question and receive private analysis, but may
not purchase a predetermined conclusion. Public artifacts must disclose material
commercial relationships and preserve the same source and claim standards.

## Release Gate

No API, benchmark, or formal sovereignty score should be sold until its data
coverage, methodology, maintenance process, and limitations are documented and
tested.
